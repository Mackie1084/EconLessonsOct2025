üè≠ What is Supply?

Supply represents the quantity of a good or service that producers are willing and able to offer at various prices during a specific time period.

üìä Basic Supply Curve

Key Point: As price increases, quantity supplied typically increases (Law of Supply)

Supply Decrease: When the entire curve shifts leftward, less is supplied at every price level
